Our Story

Where Every Chip Tells a Story

Kemsoj Bites was born from a deep love for the crisp, golden goodness of perfectly fried plantain chips. We source only the finest, ripest plantains, slice them with precision, and season them with spice blends that honour West African culinary heritage.


Every bag you open is a promise — no shortcuts, no compromises, no artificial fillers. Just real plantains, real flavour, and real pride in every bite. Whether you're snacking solo or gifting someone special, Kemsoj Bites delivers an experience worth savouring.
